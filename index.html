About Me

Hi, my name is Rohan. I discovered my passion for cybersecurity when I was young initially through game exploits. However, it was only until 2021 that I actively pursued it. Since then, I've explored the many aspects of it, pursued a Diploma in Cybersecurity & Digital Forensics, embarked on a couple of projects as well as gained various certifications. Through this journey of exploring, I have also developed a strong interest in programming and how I can use it to solve many of the problems I face. I am a strategic thinker with effective communication and social skills. I also have an inquisitive mind. I love to learn new skills and step out of my comfort zone. I believe these are all unique, valuable abilities of mine that makes me who I am. Over the years, I've also developed a strong determination to succeed in all that I do and to constantly pursue excellence.
